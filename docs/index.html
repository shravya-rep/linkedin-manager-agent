How it works
Your personal content firewall

LinkLens sits between you and your LinkedIn feed. It never posts, never clicks, never interacts — it only watches and filters.

1

You scroll normally

LinkLens watches posts appear in your feed via a browser extension — just like an ad blocker.

2

JudgeAgent scores each post

Claude AI classifies every post: topic, signal score, anxiety score. Runs locally in milliseconds.

3

Noise disappears silently

Hustle posts, anxiety content, mass hiring blasts — they collapse to a thin line. You never see them.

4

Signal gets highlighted

Agentic AI, RAG, MLOps posts get a blue highlight. You stop and read exactly what matters.

5

Daily digest on demand

At any time, request your digest. Everything you saw, organized into Tech, Jobs, People — plus what was blocked.
